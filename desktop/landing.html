Rhizome Architecture

Like a forest, underground.

01
Discovers
myc finds other myc devices on your Wi-Fi automatically. Like mycorrhizal threads reaching toward nearby roots. Zero configuration.
02
Connects
Direct device-to-device connection. No server in the middle. Powered by WebRTC—the same standard that handles real-time video calls.
03
Grows
The more devices join, the stronger the network. Weak phone + strong laptop = better AI together. Every node makes it smarter.
